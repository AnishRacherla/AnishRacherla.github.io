My Education Journey

Sanghamitra School (LKG – 10th)

I studied from LKG to 10th grade at Sanghamitra School, Hyderabad. It gave me a strong foundation in academics, discipline, and extracurricular activities. I consistently performed well in academics and participated in many school events. The school's environment helped me grow into a confident and curious learner.

Sri Chaitanya Junior College (11th – 12th)

I pursued my intermediate (11th and 12th) at Sri Chaitanya Junior College, focused on preparing for engineering entrance exams. With dedicated efforts and guidance from faculty, I secured a JEE Mains Rank: 1309 and JEE Advanced Rank: 4000. My intermediate percentage was 98%. This phase was intense but rewarding.

IIIT Hyderabad (B.Tech CSE)

Currently, I am pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Hyderabad. It's a place that encourages innovation and hands-on learning. I’m exploring fields like coding, electronics, and real-world projects. The tech-focused environment is helping me sharpen my skills and grow as an engineer.
